Our Story

My wife and I grew up in Colombia, where starting the day with world-class coffee was our daily ritual. Since moving here, we’ve missed that perfect cup. Every time we visit home, we bring bags back—and so many friends ask us to bring some for them, too.

That’s why I’m starting Ritual Specialty Coffee. It's a project close to our hearts, bringing the authentic taste of Colombian coffee directly to you.

About Our Coffee

Ritual Specialty Coffee

  • SCA Rating: Balanced 87 SCA, premium specialty coffee
  • Tasting Notes: Tropical notes, red fruits, chocolate, blueberries, silky and creamy body, fruity finish with good character and subtle wine-like notes. Balanced acidity with a prolonged and juicy aftertaste.
  • Variety: Castillo variety from Valle del Cauca
  • Process: Washed
  • Roast: Medium
  • Altitude: Grown at 1,550–1,900m above sea level
