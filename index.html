About Ritual

At Ritual, we believe every morning deserves to start with something extraordinary. That’s why we offer a premium specialty coffee crafted for those who appreciate quality in every cup.

Our coffee is of the exquisite Castillo variety, grown in the nutrient-rich soils of Valle del Cauca, Colombia—one of the world’s most celebrated coffee regions. Cultivated at elevations between 1,550 and 1,900 meters, these high-altitude beans develop complex, vibrant notes that reflect their origin.

What does an 87 SCA score mean?

The Specialty Coffee Association (SCA) scores coffee on a scale of 0–100. Only coffees that score 80 and above earn the “specialty” label. At 87, Ritual ranks among the top-tier beans globally—offering a refined flavor profile with clarity, sweetness, and balance that connoisseurs expect from exceptional coffee.

  • 87 SCA-rated, premium specialty coffee
  • Castillo variety from Valle del Cauca
  • Grown at 1,550–1,900m
